About ngNuggets

What is ngNuggets?

ngNuggets is updated daily with some of the latest/best Angular videos, blogs, podcasts and more. These are hastily curated for the Angular developer who wants a quick/easy place to find something new to learn.


Roadmap

ngNuggets is currently in the MVP stage, really just a personal project for learning purposes and for me to stay on top of Angular resources.

A couple things that I plan to include on this site are: search for posts by tag (show only posts on a certain topic), and filter posts by type (show just videos, just blog posts, etc).
